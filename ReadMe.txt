Despription:
My game is mario themed.
Player: Mario 

COLLECTABLES:
Coins:
Coins collected will increase the player's points by 20. 
1-UP Mushroom (Shroom): 
Mushrooms incerease your health by 1 and also incerease the player's points by 20.

ENEMIES:

Goombas: 
A Goomba takes off 1 health from the player if the player collides with the Goomba.
A Goomba cannot fly and will only walk on the ground.
Koopas:
A Koopa takes off 2 health from the player if the player collides with the Koopa.
A Koopa has wings and can fly. 
Bullet Bill:
A Bullet Bill will kill the player instantly if the player collides with the Bullet Bill.
A Bullet Bill can fly.

Details:
You first start off on level 1 with 0 points and every 500 points 
the speed of the game increases by 20.
The starting speed is 100 and the max speed is 300.
The max amount of health a player can have is 5.

            Speeds:         Points:
Level 1:    100             0
Level 2:    120             500
Level 3:    140             1000
Level 4:    160             1500
Level 5:    180             2000
Level 6:    200             2500
Level 7:    220             3000
Level 8:    240             3500
Level 9:    260             4000
Level 10:   280             4500
Level 11:   300             5000

Winning Conditions:
If you get 5000 points you win.

Losing conditions:
You lose if you lose all of your lives.
